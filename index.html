AMSAA Internship

Constructed machine learning models to predict Probability of Damage of various missiles for warfighter use. Achieved target performance on missile-vs-personnel data. Provided recommendations for FY19 to achieve similar performance on missile-vs-building and missile-vs-vehicle data.
